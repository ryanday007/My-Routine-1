About Me

I'm Ryan, a part-time video producer based in Chico, CA. I love creating content that tells a story and connects with audiences. When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me exploring the outdoors or trying out new recipes in the kitchen.

 

Steps In A Video Shoot

For me, a typical video project involves five basic steps.

  1. I decide what recipe to feature the night before the shoot.
  2. The day of the shoot, I make a trip to the farmer's market and the grocery store to pick up any last-minute ingredients.
  3. The actual shoot begins by staging all the ingredients for my dinner and setting up my camera in my shitty little kitchen
  4. I shoot all videaos in one take without a script. I can do all the editing in post production.
  5. Next is editing the recording, adding any text I might want, and uploading to Youtube.

The results may not be slick or ultra-professional, but I get a sense of satisfaction when they're done.
